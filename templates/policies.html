Important information

How Urnix supports tournament organizers

Urnix is built to help with tournament planning, match coordination, participant support, and event logistics. It can suggest next steps and troubleshoot common issues, but users should verify critical details.

  • Use Urnix for guidance on schedule changes, venue setup, player check-in, bracket updates, and result tracking.
  • Do not rely on Urnix for legal, safety, or financial decisions without independent confirmation.
  • Never share passwords, payment details, or private competitor information in prompts.
  • Cross-check rules, timing, and official outcomes with your event documentation when precision matters.

Recommended actions

If you face a problem during an event, consult your team, verify the source of truth, and use Urnix as a helpful assistant rather than the final decision-maker.
