Important information
Urnix Policies

Urnix is an assistant built for tournament organizers, players, and event staff. It helps with planning, coordination, and quick answers during events — but it works alongside your judgment, not in place of it. Please read the points below before relying on it during a live event.

What Urnix can help with
  • Scheduling — clarifying match times, round changes, and venue setup.
  • Check-in & brackets — guidance on player check-in flow and bracket updates.
  • On-the-day troubleshooting — suggesting next steps when something goes wrong during an event.
  • General questions — rules clarifications, logistics, and quick lookups.
What to double-check yourself
  • Safety, legal, or financial calls — Urnix should never be the final authority on these.
  • Match results & rules — always confirm against your official event documents when accuracy really matters.
  • Anything Urnix seems unsure about — treat it as a starting point, and verify with your team.
Keep private information private
  • Never share passwords, payment details, or card numbers in chat.
  • Avoid pasting confidential attendee or competitor information.
  • If something feels sensitive, it probably doesn't belong in the chat box.
If something goes wrong mid-event

Pause, ask a teammate for a second opinion, and confirm the plan with whoever owns the final call for that decision. Use Urnix to move faster and think things through — not as the single source of truth.

Urnix can make mistakes. Always verify anything critical before acting on it.
